Online Education

Advent of the Atomic Bomb

CORE 138S: Advent of the Atomic Bomb (Spring 2015)

CORE 138S focused on integrating science and history while examining the scientific evolution of nuclear weapons and the historical context in which they were developed. I worked on constructing new mechanisms to increase engagement with the material and with other users, as well as building modules about atomic physics and special relativity for a general audience. Specifically, I managed the organization and release of all course content, and created tools in edX to assist students navigate the course and communicate with alumni. Some of these included experiments with an interactive calendar, a WordPress site to curate class content, and video "postcards" from students and alumni to one another.

Emerging Global Challenges

FSEM 144: Emerging Global Challenges (Fall 2015)

This effort involved implementing the first-ever online course designed for children by university students taking a course designed, in turn, by an all-student team. I helped design the course offered by Colgate University (FSEM 144), and implement the course (breadX) that was designed by students in the Fall.
